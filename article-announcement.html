Platform Has Moved to a New Domain

Announcement Published: May 8, 2026 Read time: ~2 min
Official Announcement. This page confirms our platform's domain change. If you arrived here looking for the previous domain, you are in the right place — the platform, team, and every service are now operating from the new address.

What Changed

Only the domain name. We have rebranded the public-facing address of the service to consolidate everything under a single, clearer name. The platform itself — the member dashboard, processing engine, documentation, and support — is unchanged.

What Stayed the Same

  • Core platform — same software, same dashboard, same login credentials.
  • All existing flows — every workflow you used previously works identically on the new domain.
  • Documentation library — every published article is mirrored on the new domain.
  • Pricing, packages, member accounts — no migration required beyond updating your bookmarks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this still the same company?

Yes. The platform, the team, and the services operate exactly as before. Nothing about the underlying technology has changed — only the public-facing domain name.

What do I need to update on my end?

Replace any saved bookmark, link, or internal reference from the old domain to the new one. The same applies to the documentation subdomain and the API subdomain. No password reset, no account migration, no re-authentication is required.

Why move to a new domain?

To consolidate the platform under a clearer brand. Existing services continue without interruption on the new domain.

Next Steps

Visit the new home and update your bookmarks. If you previously linked to the old domain from internal documentation, partner portals, or saved references, please point those links to the matching path on the new domain.
